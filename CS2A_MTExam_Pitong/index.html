Bago Resort — A Natural Escape in Salcedo

Bago Resort in Salcedo, Ilocos Sur, features the province's first man-made pool filled with natural spring
water from Mount Madarang. It offers two pools for kids and adults, with clean, cold, chlorine-free water.
Surrounded by mountains and the Buwaya River, the resort provides a peaceful natural setting with cottages and picnic areas for relaxation.

Quirino Skyline View Deck — Panoramic Views of Ilocos Sur

The Quirino Skyline View Deck in Ilocos Sur offers stunning panoramic views of mountains and a sea of clouds.
It's a peaceful spot popular for sunrises, sunsets, and photography. Surrounded by lush landscapes,
the deck provides a serene escape from the city.

#QuirinoSkyline, #IlocosSurViews, #SeaOfClouds, #SunriseSunsetSpot, #NaturePhotography

Aw-asen Falls, Sigay

Aw-asen Falls in Sto. Rosario, Sigay, Ilocos Sur, is the tallest waterfall in the Ilocos Region at about 110-120 meters high.
Surrounded by untouched nature, it features fresh spring water that flows into a deep basin.
Visitors can reach this serene spot after a 45-minute trek.
